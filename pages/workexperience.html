Networking Internship at Unity Point Health

Info:

This internship at Unity Point was 6 months long and was focused on learning about and working with computer networking. I worked with the network team to help maintain the hospital's network infrastructure, which included tasks such as monitoring network performance, troubleshooting network issues, and assisting with network upgrades and expansions. I also had the opportunity to learn about various networking technologies and protocols, such as TCP/IP, DNS, DHCP, and VLANs.

The main project I worked on during my internship was a network upgrade project where I helped to upgrade the hospital's network infrastructure to support higher bandwidth and improved performance. This involved tasks such as configuring and testing new network equipment, updating network documentation, and assisting with the deployment of the new network infrastructure. Additionally, I walked through several hospitals and performed a wifi signal strength survey to help identify areas of weak wifi coverage and recommend solutions for improving wifi performance in those areas.
